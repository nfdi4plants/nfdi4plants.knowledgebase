:::note
Before you can publish your ARC to DataPLANT's publication service (based on Invenio), it needs to be validated. Follow [this guide](/nfdi4plants.knowledgebase/guides/validate-arc) to validate your ARC for Invenio. 
:::


<Card icon="gitlab" title="PLANTDataHUB">
    <Steps>

    1. Sign in to the [PLANTDataHUB](https://git.nfdi4plants.org)
    2. Navigate to the ARC you want to publish
    3. In the right sidebar click on the `Invenio` badge.

        :::tip
        You are redirected to the DataPLANT Publication Service (https://archigator.nfdi4plants.org).
        :::

    4. Login with your DataPLANT account

        :::tip
        A summary of the ARC is shown including the investigation metadata.
        :::
  
    6. You can follow the steps in the right sidebar to publish your ARC.

    </Steps>
</Card>

:::tip
Find out more about ARC data publications [here](/nfdi4plants.knowledgebase/datahub/data-publications)
:::
