## Add validation to your ARC

<Card icon="pen" title="ARCitect">
    <Steps>

    1. Open your ARC in ARCitet
    2. In the sidebar, go to the `Validation` menu
    3. In the section "Custom Validation Packages", select the "invenio" package and click `Save`
    4. Commit your changes via the `Commit` menu
    5. Upload your changes via the `DataHUB Sync` menu
    6. Open your ARC in the DataHUB

    </Steps>
</Card>

## Check the validation results

<Card icon="gitlab" title="PLANTDataHUB">
    <Steps>

    1. Sign in to the [PLANTDataHUB](https://git.nfdi4plants.org)
    2. Navigate to the validated ARC
    3. In the right sidebar you find several validation badges (e.g. `arc specification` and `invenio`)
    4. Click on the `arc specification` badge to explore the test results.

    </Steps>
</Card>

:::tip
Find out more about ARC validation [here](/nfdi4plants.knowledgebase/arc-validation/cqc-pipelines/)
:::
