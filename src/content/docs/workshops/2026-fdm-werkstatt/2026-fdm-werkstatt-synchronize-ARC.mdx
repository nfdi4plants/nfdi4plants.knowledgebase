<ViolaSays>
All the changes I made with the eLab2ARC tool are only visible in the DataHUB. How can I synchronize them with my local ARC?  
</ViolaSays>

## Download your ARC from the PLANTDataHUB

<Card icon="pen" title="ARCitect">
    <Steps>
    1. From the sidebar, navigate to the <QIcon name="published_with_changes" /> `DataHUB Sync` menu.
    2. Click `Pull` to download your ARC from the DataHUB.     
    
    </Steps>
</Card>

## Inspect ARC changes in ARCitect

<Card icon="pen" title="ARCitect">
    <Steps>
    1. Click through the new studys and assays in the sidebar.
    2. Inspect the new files and check the isa.study and isa.assay sheets to see the added metadata.
    3. Check the isa.assay file also to see the LLM extracted metadata.
  
    </Steps>
</Card>
